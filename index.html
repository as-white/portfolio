❛ We will either find a way or make one. ❜


Hi! My name is Abigail, but most of my friends call me Abby. I'm a twenty-six year-old Web Development student at Eleven Fifty Academy. I love graphic design, singing, cooking, reading and writing—I love creating art, even if that art isn't always pretty. One of my favorite parts of web development is being able to bring my ideas to life.

I first developed an interest in coding when I was thirteen years old; Myspace was the new big thing, and everyone wanted to have the prettiest pages with the best layouts. I was one of those people, studying the code, dissecting it, finding the cogs that made things tick. It was all fascinating to me, but I never felt like I was smart enough to tackle it by myself. This journey through web development has taught me how incredibly wrong I was.

Through Eleven Fifty's part-time Web Development course, I'm learning to utilize a number of skills (HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript) to build the best arsenal I possibly can. It's taught me patience, perseverance, and, above all else, the dedication that I thought I lacked for so long.

Thank you for choosing to take this journey with me!
